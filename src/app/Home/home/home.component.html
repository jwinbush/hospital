Healthcare Services

Emergency Care Surgical Services Maternity Care Intensive Care Unit (ICU) Diagnostic Imaging Outpatient Services Laboratory Services Rehabilitation Services

Emergency Care

Our Emergency Care department is equipped to handle critical situations 24/7. Our skilled team of doctors and nurses are ready to provide immediate care for all types of emergencies, ensuring that patients receive the best possible treatment quickly.

Surgical Services

Our Surgical Services include a wide range of procedures from minimally invasive surgery to complex operations. Our experienced surgeons and state-of-the-art operating rooms ensure the highest standards of care.

Maternity Care

Our Maternity Care offers a comprehensive program from prenatal care to delivery and postpartum support. We are committed to ensuring a safe and comfortable experience for both mother and baby.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

The Intensive Care Unit (ICU) provides specialized care for patients with severe or life-threatening illnesses and injuries. Our multidisciplinary team uses advanced technology to monitor and treat patients 24 hours a day.

Diagnostic Imaging

Our Diagnostic Imaging services offer a comprehensive range of tests including X-rays, MRI, CT scans, and ultrasounds. These tools help in the accurate diagnosis and treatment planning for various conditions.

Outpatient Services

Our Outpatient Services provide a variety of treatments and procedures that do not require an overnight stay. From minor surgeries to therapy sessions, we ensure a smooth and convenient experience for our patients.

Laboratory Services

Our Laboratory Services offer a full range of tests including blood work, biopsies, and other diagnostic services. Our labs are equipped with the latest technology to provide fast and accurate results.

Rehabilitation Services

Our Rehabilitation Services help patients recover from surgery, injury, or illness. We offer physical, occupational, and speech therapy tailored to each patient’s needs to restore their health and improve their quality of life.
